# HR-Attrition-Dashboard
The HR Attrition dashboard provides a detailed analysis of workforce dynamics and employee well-being. The Demographics report summarizes key employee statistics such as age groups, gender, distance from home, and marital status, offering insights into diversity and commuting patterns. Turnover Analysis I and II focus on employee attrition, analyzing departures by department, job roles, business travel impact, job level, and performance metrics. The Employee Wellness section evaluates factors affecting employee well-being, emphasizing the importance of a healthy workforce for productivity and retention.
